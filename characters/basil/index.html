Characters / Basil

Created: 2025.03.23
Last updated: 2026.04.18

Olivia and Basil are fauns, siblings, and mathematicians. They are inseparable, often working together and always getting up to hijinks together. They're highly competitive, egging each other on into more and more difficult challenges and absurd pranks.

Basil is olivia's little brother, slightly less academically gifted but making up for it in attitude and work ethic. He's also the wilder of the two personalities, usually the inciting force when they get into trouble.

Relationships

  • Olivia: sister.
  • Aava: friend. Originally met due to Aava's father Vetten (design TBD), who is Olivia and Basil's supervising professor. Basil had a brief crush on Aava, but quickly decided neither was the other's type; now he's content to needle Olivia about the obvious sexual tension between her and Aava that she stubbornly refuses to acknowledge (but doesn't deny either).
