Characters / Salma

Created: 2026.04.26
Last updated: 2026.04.26

Salma is a bit of a "lone wolf" of a merman, often found fishing or just wandering the waters by his lonesome. He's quieter and less flashy than most of his compatriots, but still enjoys a bit of fashion (he particularly likes his tattoos) and sometimes sings when he thinks nobody is listening.
