Characters / Ivu

Created: 2024.12.02
Last updated: 2026.04.26

The dryads, Ivu amongst them, are caretakers of the forest and some of the most powerful magical beings in the world, wielding shapeshifting growth and destructive fire to drive nature's cycles of death and rebirth ever onward.

Ivu is the avatar of the birch tree and also the protagonist of my long-term game project Flamegrower — in fact, the dryads' magic powers were created out of the game's mechanics.
