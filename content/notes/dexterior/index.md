An experimental collection of [dexterior](https://codeberg.org/molentum/dexterior)
simulations built for WebGL.

<!-- more -->

Split onto separate pages to avoid blowing up your computer.
Common controls: `P` to pause,
`Space` to step forward one timestep while paused.
No mouse controls at least for now, sorry to any mobile users
finding their way here.
(All of these should run on mobile too!
You just don't have access to controls.)
