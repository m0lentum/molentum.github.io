Another Inktober where I participated every other day; great fun as always!
Featuring a little substory about Miriel's trip to an abandoned city,
some less serious doodles depending on the mood of the day,
and at least one appearance of every character I've designed so far.

<!-- more -->

Previous years: [2024](/gallery/2024/inktober-2024/), [2023](/gallery/2023/inktober-2023/)

## 08. Reckless
![Miriel climbing onto a pile of rubble.
Behind the pile we see ruins of tall urban buildings overgrown with vegetation,
and in the middle of it all a large building with a giant domed roof.
There's a car under the rubble.
Miriel is carrying her bow with the string on and a quiver full of arrows,
suggesting she's worried about who might be lurking in these streets.](reckless.jpg)
Few have ventured into the old country in the centuries since the war,
and fewer still returned without scars,
but Miriel's curiosity was always stronger than her sense of self-preservation.
