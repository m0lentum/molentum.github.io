A little painting for an exhibition
organized by the local university students' art club.
Its theme is "emotions", and naturally I chose my favorite of them all,
that sense of absolute peace you can only feel when completely alone
and in no hurry to go anywhere or do anything
(for which, curiously, there's no word in the Finnish language,
but "solitude" describes it well).
