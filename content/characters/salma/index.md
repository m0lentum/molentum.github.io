Salma is a bit of a "lone wolf" of a [merman],
often found fishing or just wandering the waters by his lonesome.
He's quieter and less flashy than most of his compatriots,
but still enjoys a bit of fashion (he particularly likes his tattoos)
and sometimes sings when he thinks nobody is listening.

<!-- more -->

One day while fishing, Salma encountered [Miriel],
who was similarly in the process of fishing by herself.
The two decided to collaborate and quickly fell in love.
