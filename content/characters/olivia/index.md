Olivia and [Basil] are [fauns], siblings, and mathematicians.
They are inseparable, often working together
and always getting up to hijinks together.
They're highly competitive, egging each other on
into more and more difficult challenges and absurd pranks.

Olivia is Basil's older sister, two years ahead in her studies
and usually the victor in their academic contests.
She's more reserved in personality than her brother, though,
and doesn't always go along with his wildest ideas.
